How it works

Deskplay works in two parts: an Android app and a desktop app. The Android app organizes and manages access to your game library. The desktop app is a desktop frontend responsible for launching and controlling games from your Android on your computer. Download the Android app on your device, then download and install the desktop app on your computer. From the desktop app, simply connect your Android device to the USB port, choose your game and start playing.

Features

Launch Android games, emulators or any app on your PC. Forward Android audio to your PC

Use the built-in game touch mapper

Turn off the Android screen or use another app while playing

Use your favorite game launcher app as the frontend
